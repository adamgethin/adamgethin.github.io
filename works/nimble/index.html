Nimble is a WolframAlpha® menubar client for macOS, released by Maybulb.

UI design and logo design for an open-source, third-party WolframAlpha® menubar client for macOS, plus design and development of a responsive product website.

The app was downloaded over 10,000 times since its release in early 2016 after being featured by technology news sites The Next Web and Lifehacker, as well as on the front page of Product Hunt.

Nimble has been deprecated as of June 2020.
